About

Welcome to Terreno Club, a private sanctuary in the heart of Palma’s historic El Terreno district. Designed for those who appreciate the balance between sport, relaxation, and refined social living, Terreno Club offers an exclusive setting where Mediterranean elegance meets contemporary comfort.

Members can enjoy padel courts, a vibrant poolside terrace, and a restaurant that blends sophistication with an easy, natural charm. Whether for an early match, a leisurely lunch, or an evening among friends, Terreno Club invites you to slow down and savour life’s finer moments.

Padel

Enjoy three premium padel courts designed for doubles play in a bright, open-air setting. Play matches with friends or participate in community games while enjoying the club’s atmosphere.

Members may also book private or group lessons with our professional coaches for tailored guidance and skill development.

Food

Our restaurant is open to the public, serves breakfast and lunch made from locally sourced ingredients alongside smoothies and specialty coffee.

On weekends and public holidays, enjoy our special brunch offerings.

We also host seasonal events throughout the year.

Pool

Immerse yourself in our heated saltwater outdoor pool, surrounded by lush Mediterranean greenery and lounging areas that offer an escape from the city’s rhythm.

Unwind on sunbeds as you enjoy refreshing drinks and wholesome snacks from our restaurant.

Enhance your experience with outdoor showers, fresh towels, and a serene atmosphere designed to rejuvenate you at any time of day.

Memberships

Discover a place where wellness, leisure, and connection come naturally.

As a member, you enjoy:

  • Our heated saltwater pool
  • Unlimited padel court use at no extra cost
  • 10% savings at the restaurant and poolside bar
  • Guest invitations at 10 € per person/day
  • Priority access to curated events and exclusive member experiences
  • No joining fee

Annual Membership (1,290 €): Full access to all club facilities and member privileges.

Household Membership (967.50 €): Add household members at a 25% reduced rate, with full benefits included.

Children:

  • 0–5 years: Complimentary
  • 6–13 years: 430 €/year or 10 €/day guest access
  • 14+ years: 645 €/year or 10 €/day guest access
