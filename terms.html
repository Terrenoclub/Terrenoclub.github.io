Terms & Conditions

Last updated: 18 August 2025

Welcome to Terreno Club. By becoming a member, you agree to the following Terms & Conditions.

1. Membership & Benefits

  • Full access to member areas, including the heated pool
  • Unlimited use of padel courts at no extra charge
  • 10% discount at the restaurant and poolside bar
  • Guest/kids invites available at 10 €/guest/day
  • Priority access to special events, activities, and other member perks
  • No joining fee currently applies

2. Membership Categories & Pricing

  • Annual Membership: 1,290 € (equivalent to 107.5 €/month), payable upfront
  • Household Advantage: Each additional household member can join at 25% off (967.5 €/year), with full benefits
  • Children:
    • Ages 0–5: Free
    • Ages 6–13: 70% discount (430 €/year) or occasional guest access (10 €/day)
    • Ages 14+: Household membership at 50% off (645 €/year) or guest access (10 €/day)

Memberships are personal and non-transferable. Pricing is subject to change with 30 days’ written notice.

3. Payments & Renewals

  • Memberships are payable annually in advance
  • Auto-renewal: Memberships automatically renew unless cancelled with 7 days’ written notice before expiration
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer, credit card (online), or other approved methods
  • Fees are non-refundable except as required by law
  • Late payments: A 7-day grace period applies; failure to pay may result in suspension or cancellation of membership
  • Refund policy: Pro-rated refunds may be considered for special cases (subject to documentation)

4. Guests & Visitor Policy

  • Guests must always be accompanied by a member and register upon entry
  • Guest access: 10 €/person/day, payable in advance
  • Guest limits: Maximum 4 guests per member at any time (based on availability); please inquire for bigger groups
  • Members are fully responsible for their guests’ behavior and compliance with Club Rules
  • Repeated violations by guests may result in member sanctions

5. Use of Facilities

  • Members and guests must respect Club Rules and Code of Conduct at all times
  • Booking requirements: Padel courts and certain facilities require advance booking through our system
  • Operating hours: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM daily
  • The Club may introduce booking systems or time limits on facilities during peak demand
  • The Club reserves the right to close areas temporarily for maintenance, private events, or safety reasons
  • Quiet zones: Members must pass through communal areas quietly and promptly, respecting neighboring residents

5.1 Parking

  • Parking spaces are provided for the convenience of members and their guests during club visits
  • Parking is limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Time limits: Parking is restricted to active club usage periods, for a maximum of 4 hours; overnight parking is prohibited without prior written approval
  • Guest parking: Guest vehicles must display temporary parking permits available at reception
  • The Club is not responsible for damage, theft, or loss related to vehicles or contents in parking areas
  • Members are responsible for their guests’ parking compliance
  • Improperly parked or abandoned vehicles may be towed at owner’s expense

6. Code of Conduct

  • Respectful behavior towards staff, members, and guests is mandatory
  • Appropriate attire required in all areas (swimwear only in pool area)
  • No smoking in indoor areas; designated outdoor smoking areas only
  • Noise levels must be kept reasonable, especially in transition areas
  • Use of mobile devices should be considerate of others
  • Club property must be treated with care
