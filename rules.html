House Rules & Code of Conduct

At Terreno Club we aim to create a warm, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all members and guests. These rules help us maintain the quality of our facilities and ensure that everyone feels at home.

By becoming a member or visiting as a guest, you agree to follow our House Rules & Code of Conduct.

1. Access & Guests

  • The Club is reserved for members and their registered guests.
  • Guest registration: All guests must check in and pay the day pass (10€) at reception before entering.
  • All guests must be accompanied by the member who invited them at all times.
  • Only members may invite guests (maximum 4 guests per member).
  • Children without a membership will be considered guests and subject to guest fees.
  • Equipment rental: Rackets and towels for guests can be rented at the restaurant or pool bar.
  • Child supervision: Children under 12 must be supervised by a parent or responsible adult at all times; a parental consent form must be signed for unaccompanied minors 12-15.

2. Using the Facilities

  • Opening hours: 09:00 – 21:00 daily, unless announced otherwise.
  • Court booking: Courts can be booked via the reservation system or WhatsApp; cancellations require 24 hours’ notice. Only members can reserve courts.
  • Booking limits: 2 hours per member per day (extensions possible subject to availability).
  • Club boundaries: Please remain within designated club areas at all times.
  • Communal areas: When passing through shared community spaces, please keep noise to a minimum and avoid lingering. We appreciate your cooperation in respecting our neighbors.
  • Pool area:
    • No lifeguard is on duty; swim at your own risk.
    • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult in the pool area.
    • No running or diving.
    • Shared amenities: Sunbeds, loungers, and shared spaces cannot be “reserved” for extended periods when not in use.

3. Dress Code & Safety

  • Court attire: Suitable sportswear required when using courts.
  • Footwear: Padel-specific shoes are mandatory for safety and court protection; street shoes prohibited on courts.
  • Pool attire: Appropriate swimwear required in pool areas; no street clothes in pool.
  • Liability: The Club cannot take responsibility for injuries caused by unsuitable footwear or equipment.

4. Courtesy & Respect

  • Treat fellow members, guests, and staff with kindness and respect.
  • Zero tolerance: Unsportsmanlike behavior, aggression, harassment, or verbal/physical abuse will result in immediate suspension.
  • Member responsibility: Members are fully responsible for their guests’ behavior and any violations.
  • Noise consideration: Keep conversations at reasonable levels, especially near residential areas.

5. Equipment & Club Property

  • Care of equipment: Please handle Club equipment (rackets, towels, cushions, loungers, etc.) with care.
  • Damage policy: Members may be charged for repairs or replacements if damage occurs through misuse or negligence.
  • Return policy: All borrowed equipment must be returned to reception before departure.

5.1 Parking

  • Active use only: Park only while actively using Club facilities; extended or overnight parking prohibited.
  • Time limits: Maximum parking duration of 4 hours during club operating hours.
  • Designated spaces: Park only in marked Club parking areas; respect disabled parking zones.
  • Guest vehicles: All guest cars must obtain temporary parking permits from reception.
  • No storage: Parking areas cannot be used for vehicle storage or non-Club related activities.
  • Access consideration: Do not block access ways, emergency exits, or other vehicles.
  • Abandoned vehicles: Vehicles left overnight or beyond reasonable usage periods may be towed at owner’s cost.
  • Responsibility: Members are liable for their guests’ parking violations and any associated costs.

6. Food, Drinks & Substances

  • Outside policy: Outside food and drinks are not permitted without prior Club approval.
  • Alcohol: Alcoholic beverages may only be purchased from and consumed in designated Club areas.
  • Prohibited substances: Illegal substances are strictly forbidden on Club premises.
  • Glass policy: No glass containers in pool areas for safety reasons.

7. Pets

  • Welcome policy: Well-behaved pets are welcome with constant supervision.
  • Restricted areas: For hygiene and safety, pets are prohibited in pool areas, changing rooms, and restaurant.
  • Owner responsibility: Owners must clean up after pets immediately and ensure they don’t disturb other members.
  • Leash requirement: Pets must remain leashed or under voice control at all times.

8. Cleanliness & Maintenance

  • Shared responsibility: Help keep communal areas (changing rooms, restrooms, poolside, etc.) clean and tidy.
  • Waste disposal: Use bins provided and dispose of waste properly.
  • Towel service: Return used towels to designated baskets.
  • Personal belongings: The Club is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

9. Privacy & Photography

  • Photography policy: Photography/filming is permitted for personal use only; respect others’ privacy.
  • Social media: Please be considerate when posting images that include other members.
  • Commercial use: Professional photography or filming requires prior written permission.

10. Incidents & Enforcement

  • Reporting: Please report any issues, accidents, or concerns to Club staff immediately.
  • Feedback welcome: We encourage suggestions to improve your Club experience.
  • Violation consequences: From warning and possible temporary suspension, to Membership suspension or termination in the worst cases.

Emergency Procedures

  • Emergency contacts: [Emergency numbers posted throughout facility].
  • First aid: Basic first aid supplies available at reception.
  • Evacuation: In case of emergency, follow staff instructions and use designated exits.
  • Incident reporting: All accidents must be reported to staff and documented.

Thank you for helping us keep Terreno Club a relaxed, respectful, and friendly place for everyone.

These rules are subject to periodic updates. Members will be notified of any significant changes.
